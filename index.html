COVID-19

What is it and My experience with COVID-19

COVID-19, is a virus that was first reported on the 31st of December 2019, just the day before new years day. It was first reported in Wuhan, China. Right now there are over 5.7 6.3 million deaths from just 1 and a half years. When I was in Year 7, attending Langley Grammar School, there was a massive outbreak, which led to us doing online school. This had major advantages and disadvantages. One advantage is that we got to make our work environment as comfortable as possible. We also dont forget anything as it is right in out house. A disadvantage though is that you miss being in a social place where you can interact with new people and enjoy your life as compared to sitting at home, where you just stare at a screen all day from 8am to 3pm with small ten minute breaks in between. Right now I am fine but before I missed doing things in person. But a good plus side is that I got to spend more time with my family, especially my sister.
